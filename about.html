Some things about Stephanie


Stephanie has been retouching for over 8 years. She started as a product photographer retouching her own images for ecommerce websites and magazines and quickly fell in love with it. A lot of her work has been intense color matching of high end minerals and gemstones, designer jewelry, and luxury handbags. Always learning new techniques and tricks in the world of Photoshop, she thoroughly enjoys collaborating with groups and individuals in the business.
